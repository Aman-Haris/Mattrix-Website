About Mattrix Auto Corp

Mattrix Auto Corp is a partnership firm founded by Mr. Haris P. S. and Mr. Rejith John, both seasoned professionals with over 25 years of experience in the automobile industry. Established in 2020, the company began as a small business and has since expanded significantly, now achieving an annual turnover exceeding ₹4 crores.

The company's registered headquarters is located in Kalasipalyam New Extension, Bangalore, with a branch office on Savarline Road, Shivamogga. Business operations in Bangalore are managed by Mr. Lorence V. K., a veteran in the automobile sector with over 30 years of experience, previously associated with the Popular Group.

With a steadfast commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction, Mattrix Auto Corp continues to strengthen its presence in the automobile industry, delivering reliable solutions tailored to market demands.

Our Mission

Our mission is to achieve a turnover of ₹10 crores by the end of 2030.

Our Vision

To establish the most efficient distribution network, offering top-quality products and services at competitive rates.

Our Team

Mattrix Auto Corp is supported by a dedicated team of 25 staff members, comprising both sales and support personnel. Their collective expertise and commitment are pivotal to our ongoing success and customer satisfaction.
